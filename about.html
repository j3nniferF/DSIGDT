What is DSIGDT?

DSIGDT (Dumb Stuff I Gotta Do Today) is a productivity web application designed to help you stay focused and get things done. Built with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, tears, love and coffee.

Simply add tasks using the input field, use the focus timer to stay on track, and watch your statistics grow. Enjoy celebratory GIFs when you complete tasks or finish focus sessions!

The quote bar pulls from free APIs for affirmations and fun facts, with built-in fallback messages if those APIs are unavailable.
